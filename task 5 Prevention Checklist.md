### **Prevention Checklist**



##### **✅ Stay Safe from Ransomware**

* Do not click on unknown links or email attachments
* Always download apps/software from trusted sources
* Keep your system and antivirus updated
* Use strong and unique passwords
* Backup important data regularly
* Avoid using public or unsecured Wi-Fi
* Never share personal or financial details online
* Do not install pirated software

##### **💳 UPI / Financial Safety**

* Never share OTP or UPI PIN with anyone
* Always verify payment requests before approving

##### **🚨 Report Cybercrime**

* Website: https://www.cybercrime.gov.in
* Helpline: 1930
